Software Developer

I've never been someone to remain complacent and I'm always looking to take on new challenges. In July of 2021 after spending the last eight years in restaurant management, I decided to pursure a career in software developement . What drew me to software engineering is the problem solving it required and the potential for growth within the industry. All I need is an opportunity to make a difference.

Currently I live in Chicago, Illinois. When I'm not working on my career I enjoy spending time with my wife, Tiffany, and our two dogs. You can often find us staying active by getting outdoors, lifting weights and doing almost anything that is centered around sports or competition. I'm a die hard Denver Broncos fan, even though it hasn't been easy these past couple of years.

When my time is over in this life, my hope is that I have made a lasting impact on those around me and I left it a better place. I genuinely care about the success of other people and love being a part of the process of helping someone grow.

Portfolio

Here are a few of the projects that I have worked on.

Actualize Coding Bootcamp
Capstone Project

Personally built a web application that guides and allows beginners in weight lifting to sign up/login and generate customizable workouts based on preferences. Enables users to feel confident in their workout selection and encourages them to be better than yesterday by tracking progress over time using 3rd party API’s for statistics and charts, as well as personal comments. Backend development built using Ruby on Rails framework and Ruby programming language. The frontend development was structured using Vue.js framework to work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap.

Mini Capstone Project

A web application that mimics websites similar to Amazon, GOAT and StockX. Allows users to sign in to buy and sell Air Jordan shoes. Backend is built using Ruby programming language and Ruby on Rails framework. Web requests are then sent to the backend from the frontend using Vue.js framework and styled with a Bootstrap library.
